Smoothed Polygonal Models

Smoothed Polygons are a low res Polygonal mesh with an adjustable polySmoothFace node in their history. This method allows us to render many more models with high quality textures than most other VR apps.

Exlusively on HTC Vive

Berry.Run is coming exclusively to the HTC Vive headset. We believe the Vive is the clear future of virtual reality, and will be offering long term support. We will also be launching lite versions of the app to various Appl stores including Apple and Google playstores.

Life-like Hand Movements

We've hand crafted (no pun intended) over 20 different hand models to choose from, all with over 1o points of articulation. We want to provide only the most realistic hand gestures.
