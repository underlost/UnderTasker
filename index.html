The Journey

Get the camera ready as we cruise out of downtown Seattle’s Pier 69 and head north along the eastern coast of Whidbey Island soaking in marvelous views of the stately Cascade Range. Glide under the impressive and historic Deception Pass Bridge while keeping an eye out to spot harbor seals lounging on the rocky shoreline and beautiful bald eagles soaring overhead.

Continuing along, you’ll wind through the breathtaking channels and islands of the 10-million-year-old San Juan archipelago, featuring an array of landscapes from windswept grasslands to pine and Madrona-filled forests. To top off the adventure, you’ll stop in picturesque Friday Harbor, WA, brimming with an array of boutique shops, restaurants, galleries and museums. Quintessential Pacific Northwest!
